# Customization

This section covers advanced customization options for tailoring PuzzleSpring to your needs, including template overrides and custom styling.

## Who Is This For?

This section is for **advanced users** who want to:

- Customize the look and feel of their puzzle hunts
- Override default templates
- Add custom static files and styles
- Create custom info pages
